Disobey Challenge

A puzzle that will require many techniques to solve.

Capture the flag

Capture the Flag is bunch of different kind of challenges, ranging from easy to hard in difficulty, which you need to solve! For every completed challenge, you get some points. The team with the most points wins.

For a general calibration of the difficulty level, you can find the walkthrough for the teaser puzzle at the following link: https://goo.gl/yeL6rs

Rules:

  • Making a puzzle for the CtF Challenge does NOT invalidate you from participating!
  • Your logo can be visible in the puzzle, but you may not collect participant data
  • A puzzle typically has 3 levels in increasing difficulty and each should unlock a text flag, which we will provide for you
  • Finalized versions must run as VMs in a KVM environment or be easily replicable
  • You can submit either a VM image or, preferrably, Ansible / Chef / Puppet / Vagrant text files
  • The puzzle state must be resettable so that contestants do not permanently contaminate the state transitions
  • If you have a hardcopy puzzle or need help with the above, please contact us anyway
  • Exceptions can be made in deserving cases

Remember that good puzzles are:

  1. Reusable / resettable
  2. Do not require hours of computing power to calculate
  3. Have shortcuts and do not require tedious handwork
  4. Should not require loads of cash to solve -- this is not a pay-to-win mobile game
