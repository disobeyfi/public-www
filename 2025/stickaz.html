Sticker Design Competition

Because no cybersecurity event is complete without stickers, we want our community to be part of creating one. Design a sticker that we can hand out in our event and win some swag!

Competition Rules

Eligibility: The competition is open to everyone.

Theme: The sticker design should follow the Disobey's spirit and colour scheme.

Design Requirements:
- The sticker design must be submitted in a printable file format (e.g., PNG, SVG).
- The design should be high-resolution (300 dpi or higher) to ensure quality printing. Keep the size smallish (e.g. round design appr. 6-7 cm, rectangle appr. 8 x 4 cm).
- The design must be original and not infringe on any copyrights or trademarks.

Submission Process:
- You are free to submit max 2 designs.
- Submit your sticker design to contact@disobey.fi by 6.2.2025. The subject line of your email must include the tag [STICKER] for your submission to be considered.
- Please include your name, email address, and a brief description of your design in your submission email.

Judging:
- A panel of judges from the Disobey team will select the winning designs. The judges will select 1-3 winners.
- The judges will be looking for creativity, originality, and how well the design captures the spirit of the Disobey mindset.

Prizes:
- The winner will receive a prize pack of Disobey event swag.
- The winning designs will be printed as stickers and handed out at Disobey events.

Rights:
- By submitting a design, you grant the Disobey organizers the exclusive right to use your design for any purpose related to the Disobey event. This includes the right to reproduce, modify, and distribute your design. Disobey will only retain the rights or utilize for the designs submitted by the winners of the competition.

Important Dates
Submission deadline: 6.2.2025
Winner announcement: 10.2.2025
